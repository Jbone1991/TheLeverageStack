Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 30, 2026

The Leverage Stack is a content publication and the private automation that publishes it. This policy describes how we handle any data encountered during operation.

1. Data we collect

We do not collect personal data from visitors to this website or from any users. The publishing tool operates solely on behalf of its owner to post content to social media platforms including TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook. There is no user-facing registration, login, or data entry.

2. TikTok API data

When authenticating with TikTok, an access token is obtained via OAuth and stored locally on a private machine. This token is used only to publish videos to our own TikTok account. We do not access, store, or share any data belonging to other TikTok users.

3. Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties. Authentication credentials are stored locally and never transmitted to any external server other than the relevant platform APIs (TikTok, Meta).

4. Data retention

Access tokens are stored locally in a private environment file on the operator's machine. They are refreshed as needed and can be revoked at any time through the respective platform's developer settings.

5. Affiliate links

This site contains affiliate links. When you click one, the destination merchant may set its own cookies to attribute a purchase. We do not receive personal information from these clicks beyond aggregate, anonymized commission reporting from the affiliate networks.

6. Children's privacy

This site and tool do not interact with or knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated at any time. The effective date above reflects the most recent revision.

8. Contact

For questions, contact us at centerlinewebsolutions@gmail.com.

Data Deletion Instructions

Effective date: July 2, 2026

The Leverage Stack is a private personal automation tool used to publish content to social media platforms. This page describes how any data associated with this application can be deleted.

What Data We Hold

This tool does not collect or store personal data from any users. The only data retained is an OAuth access token for our own social media accounts, stored locally on a private machine. No data is stored on external servers controlled by this application.

How to Request Data Deletion

If you believe data associated with your account has been accessed through this application, or if you would like to revoke any connection between your Facebook or Instagram account and this app, you can do so in two ways:

  1. Revoke app access directly: Go to your Facebook Settings > Security and Login > Apps and Websites, find "The Leverage Stack", and click Remove. This immediately revokes all tokens and disconnects the app.
  2. Contact us by email: Send a deletion request to centerlinewebsolutions@gmail.com. Include your Facebook or Instagram username so we can confirm and document the request. We will respond within 30 days.

What Happens After a Request

Upon receiving a verified deletion request, any locally stored tokens or identifiers associated with your account will be permanently deleted from our systems. Because we do not operate a database or external data store, no further action is required on our end beyond revoking access.

Contact

For any questions about data deletion, contact us at centerlinewebsolutions@gmail.com.

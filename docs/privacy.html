Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 29, 2026

The Leverage Stack is a private personal automation tool. This policy describes how we handle any data encountered during operation.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data from any users. This tool operates solely on behalf of its owner to post content to social media platforms including TikTok. No user-facing registration, login, or data entry exists.

2. TikTok API Data

When authenticating with TikTok, an access token is obtained via OAuth and stored locally on a private machine. This token is used only to publish videos to our own TikTok account. We do not access, store, or share any data belonging to other TikTok users.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties. Authentication credentials are stored locally and never transmitted to any external server other than the relevant platform APIs (TikTok, Meta).

4. Data Retention

Access tokens are stored locally in a private environment file (.env) on the operator's machine. They are refreshed as needed and can be revoked at any time through the respective platform's developer settings.

5. Children's Privacy

This tool does not interact with or collect data from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated at any time. The effective date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
